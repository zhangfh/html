iOS LARGEM Help

LARGEM Watch Helper

By default, This Application will connect your LARGEM watch by BLE. The default function includes Sync your iphone's message to your watch, don't miss any import information for you.

Sync Weather for LARGEM

Set City in LARGEM Watch and fetch it by your iphone, you can know the weather information any time.

This feature can be configured by going to weather setting.

Distance Measurements

The LARGEM Watch can measurement your walk distance, caculate calories consumed , sync data to this App.

Altimeter

LARGEM Watch can get your current altimeter , only enable location in your iphone.

Camera

use LARGEM, you can control your camera by it, heterodyne is not a dream.
